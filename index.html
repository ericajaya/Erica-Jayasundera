I'm a senior software engineer and technical co-founder with 13+ years building production systems in fintech, and a PhD candidate in Adversarial Machine Learning. For the last two years I've been building, red-teaming, and evaluating LLM agents and multi-agent systems end-to-end — from prototype to production — including a formal LLM red-teaming framework mapped to the OWASP LLM Top 10 and MITRE ATLAS.

I'm comfortable moving between hands-on prompt/agent iteration, evaluation design, and translating technical findings for both engineering and product audiences. I've co-founded two ventures, owning product, technical, and customer-facing decisions across both.

My research focus is adversarial robustness in ML systems, which directly informs my applied red-teaming and evaluation-design work. I also mentor and compete at AI security hackathons, most recently the AMD Developer Hackathon and IBM Bob Hackathon.
